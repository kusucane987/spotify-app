Song Title:Finding Her – Title Track

Finding Her is a soothing, heart-touching track that captures the journey of seeking love, peace, and a sense of belonging. The song beautifully reflects the quiet moments of self-discovery, where one looks for “her” — not just as a person, but as a feeling of comfort, warmth, and home. With soft melodies and thoughtful lyrics, it expresses the solitude of wandering through life, holding onto hope that one day the right soul will cross your path. The music paints a dreamy atmosphere, like walking through clouds with the heart full of unanswered questions and silent prayers. It speaks to anyone who has ever waited for the right person — someone who heals, understands, and completes the story your heart has been writing alone. “Finding Her” is not just a love song, but an emotional search for that one connection that brings meaning to everything
