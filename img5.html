Song Title:Mera Hua (From "Ek Deewane Ki Deewaniyat")

“Mera Hua” is a soothing and heartfelt romantic track that beautifully captures the warmth of finding love that feels pure, comforting, and meant to be. The song expresses the tender emotions of realizing that someone has quietly become an inseparable part of your heart and life. Its melodious composition and gentle vocals create a peaceful, dreamy atmosphere that pulls listeners into the sweetness of true love. The lyrics reflect the magic of belonging — that special moment when two souls connect effortlessly and everything starts to feel complete. As the song flows, it paints a picture of love filled with trust, affection, and silent promises that need no words. “Mera Hua” leaves a soft emotional imprint, making listeners relive the beautiful feeling of being loved and cherished by the one who truly understands them.
