Song Title:Deewaniyat (From "Ek Deewane Ki Deewaniyat")

“Deewaniyat” is a passionate romantic track that beautifully captures the fine line between devotion and obsession in love. With Vishal Mishra’s soulful and heartfelt vocals, the song instantly connects with the listener’s emotions, while Kaushik-Guddu’s soothing yet intense composition creates a mesmerizing musical atmosphere. The lyrics by Kunaal Vermaa express the madness of love — a sweet, addictive feeling that becomes impossible to let go. It reflects longing, vulnerability, and the fear of losing someone who means everything to you. The track shows how true love, when felt deeply, transforms into a deewaniyat that takes over the heart and soul. As the music gradually intensifies, it mirrors the rising emotions of a lover’s heart, making the song deeply relatable. Overall, “Deewaniyat” leaves a lasting impression, staying with the listener long after it ends and pulling them into the memories of someone unforgettable
